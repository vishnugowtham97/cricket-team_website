Welcome to XII - VIKING

At XII - VIKING, we breathe cricket, embracing the sport's spirit of camaraderie and competition. As an integral part of the IPL circuit, we carry a legacy of excellence and triumph, adorned with prestigious accolades and memorable victories on the grand stage.


Our ethos revolves around nurturing talent and instilling sportsmanship values, making us a formidable force both on and off the field. We pride ourselves on our inclusive culture, welcoming cricket enthusiasts from all walks of life, regardless of their experience or skill level.


Join our illustrious ranks today and become a cherished member of our dynamic cricket community. Together, let's embark on a journey filled with passion, teamwork, and unforgettable moments on the cricket pitch!


Indian Premier League (IPL)
Domestic Tournament
The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, contested annually by eight franchises representing different cities. It is one of the most-watched cricket leagues globally and features top international and domestic players. The IPL is known for its high-quality cricket, entertainment, and competitive spirit.

Champion's Trophy
International Tournament
The Champion's Trophy is an international cricket tournament organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC), featuring the top eight One Day International (ODI) teams in the world. It is one of the most prestigious tournaments in the cricketing world, with intense competition and thrilling matches.

World Cup
International Tournament
The Cricket World Cup is the premier international championship of men's cricket, organized by the ICC. It is contested by the top national teams of the ICC member countries and held every four years. The World Cup is the pinnacle of achievement in cricket, with teams competing fiercely for the coveted trophy.
